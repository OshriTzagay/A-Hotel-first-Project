Some thing to know about our Hotel...

The prestigious and award-winning Herods Tel Aviv Hotel is one of Tel Aviv’s finest luxury hotels, exuding a charming old-world style that you won’t find anywhere else. Nestled in the buzzing heart of Tel Aviv, just minutes’ walk from its most famous neighborhoods, and the white sand and emerald waters of the Mediterranean, Herods Tel Aviv Hotel affords you the perfect way to experience authentic Tel Aviv. With a focus on discerning business travelers, Herods Tel Aviv Hotel is a unique themed business hotel that transports its guests back in time to the vibrant atmosphere of Tel Aviv’s White City circa the 1930s, coupled with the ultimate in modern hospitality and luxury.
